About

About the Hub

The Saudi Opportunity Hub is a free, open-access dataset and discovery tool tracking grants, tenders, accelerators, investment programs, and fellowships across 15 key sectors of Saudi Arabia's evolving economy.

Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 commitments have generated a diverse portfolio of funding and partnership opportunities across Agriculture, Energy, Healthcare, ICT, Financial Services, Tourism, and more — from government-backed programs to global accelerators actively recruiting Saudi startups, researchers, and institutions. This hub aggregates those signals in one place.

Coverage spans three tiers: Saudi programs issued by Saudi institutions (CST, RDIA, Monsha'at, KAUST, KACST, PIF-backed entities); GCC programs from UAE, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain, and Kuwait open to Saudi entities; and Global programs from international agencies, accelerators, and companies actively open to Saudi participants.

This is an independent, community-maintained project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representative of the Saudi government, any Saudi authority, or any program listed. Always verify details directly with the sponsoring organization before applying.
