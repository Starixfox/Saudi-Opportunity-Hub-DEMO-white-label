    About

    About This Hub

    The Saudi Opportunity Hub is a private demo platform tracking grants, tenders, accelerators, investment programs, and fellowships across 15 key sectors of Saudi Arabia's evolving economy.

    Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 commitments have generated a diverse portfolio of funding and partnership opportunities across Agriculture, Energy, Healthcare, ICT, Financial Services, Tourism, and more — from government-backed programs to global accelerators actively recruiting Saudi startups, researchers, and institutions. This hub aggregates those signals in one place.

    Programmes are classified by origin across three tiers: Saudi-issued — programmes from Saudi institutions including CST, RDIA, Monsha'at, KAUST, KACST, and PIF-backed entities; GCC-issued — programmes from UAE, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain, and Kuwait; and Internationally issued — global programmes from international agencies, accelerators, and companies, many of which are open or accessible to Saudi-based applicants. Origin tier (who issues the programme) is distinct from applicant eligibility (who can apply), which is tracked separately in each record.

    This is a proprietary demo. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representative of any government, authority, or program listed. Always verify details directly with the sponsoring organization.
